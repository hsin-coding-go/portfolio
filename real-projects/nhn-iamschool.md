# Real-World Delivery — NHN / iamschool Taiwan

Products and market initiatives I owned as **Product Manager** at NHN (2015–2018), leading the Taiwan market expansion of iamschool — Korea's largest school-parent communication platform.

---

## iamschool Taiwan — Market Expansion (0 → 1)

**Role:** Product Manager / Taiwan Market Lead
**Scope:** Full product lifecycle — market entry, feature localization, cross-border engineering collaboration, go-to-market execution, government partnerships

iamschool is a school-parent communication platform with near-100% adoption among Korean schools. As the sole Taiwan market owner, I built the operation from zero to 3,000+ schools, achieving 60%+ adoption among Taiwan elementary schools and reaching #1 on the App Store and Google Play (Education category) multiple times between 2015 and 2017.

---

### Cross-Border Engineering Collaboration

Worked directly with the Korea-based IT team and CTO to drive product development for the Taiwan market:

- Wrote product specifications in both Mandarin and English, translating Taiwan user requirements into technical requirements for Korean engineers
- Tracked development timelines and sprint progress across time zones, acting as the primary bridge between Taiwan business needs and Korea engineering execution
- Coordinated release cycles to align feature delivery with local school calendars and government partnership milestones

---

### Product Localization & Feature Delivery

Led end-to-end delivery of Taiwan-specific features and localization:

- Owned product planning for mobile app and web-based OPS tools, driving feature prioritization based on MAU/DAU data and user interviews
- Delivered 5+ key features tailored to Taiwan school operations, improving user engagement by 15%
- Built a reusable UI component library to improve development efficiency by ~25% and reduce future localization effort

---

### Go-to-Market & Government Partnerships

- Designed and executed go-to-market strategy from scratch, including sales motion, onboarding flow, and school adoption playbook
- Led strategic partnership with Taiwan's Ministry of Education and established MOUs with 6 regional governments, embedding iamschool as the official school communication platform across public school systems
- Delivered 30+ product presentations and demos to school administrators and government stakeholders

---
